    ABOUT

    The Sound of Music school, which has 5- Star Rating was established in 2003. We are a competitive music school conveniently located, providing the highest quality of teaching that promotes encouraged learning.

    Beginning from 2003, 170 of our students have participated in the Junior Music Festival sponsored by the National Federation of Music, with 140 of them receiving a superior rating (best achievement).

    We have a vested interest in your child’s success – offering music scholarship, and the best value for your investment. We are not an afterschool day care, we are part of your child’s essential education.

    Your children will thank you.
